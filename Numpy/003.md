## unary

> /ˈjuːnəri/	adj.[数] 一元的

##### Many **unary** operations, such as computing the sum of all the elements in the array, are implemented as methods of the `ndarray` class.

许多一元运算，例如计算数组中所有元素的总和，都作为ndarray类的方法来实现。
